Why we're different

A public utility, not a lead funnel.

During Medicare's Annual Enrollment Period, seniors are buried under TV ads, robocalls, and direct mail — most of it designed to capture a phone number and route it to a call center. BenefitClock is built to be the opposite of that.

No phone number, ever

There's no form, no "talk to a licensed agent," no callback. You get the numbers and you're done. We don't want your phone number and we'll never ask for it.

Your data stays yours

The calculators run entirely in your browser. We don't sell leads, we take no commissions from insurance companies, and we're not a broker. Read the pledge.

Straight from the source

Every number comes from public government files — CMS for Medicare, the SSA and BLS for the COLA. No spin, no "recommended" plan we're paid to push. See our data.

Mark your calendar

Two deadlines, one busy autumn.

The moment you learn your 2027 Social Security raise is almost the exact moment you're allowed to change your Medicare plan. BenefitClock is built for that overlap.
